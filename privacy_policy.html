Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which applyto.jobs collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users ("users" or "you") of our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any personal information you provide while using our website.

1. Information We Collect:

We may collect and store certain information when you use our website and services. This information includes, but is not limited to:

1.1 Personal Information:

When you submit your resume and job description to our website, we collect and store the information you provide. This may include your name, contact details, employment history, education, skills, and any other relevant information necessary to generate the customized resume, cover letter, and LinkedIn summary.

1.2. Usage Data:

We may collect non-personal information about your interactions with our website and services. This may include your IP address, browser type, device information, access times, and referring URLs. We do not use cookies or any other kind of tracking, analytics, or client state.

2. Use of Collected Information:

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1. Customized Document Creation:

The information you provide, including your resume and job description, is used to generate personalized resumes, cover letters, and LinkedIn summaries to enhance your chances of securing a job. We employ a combination of artificial intelligence and human review to ensure the accuracy and quality of the generated content.

2.2. Performance Improvement:

We may temporarily store your submitted data and responses, as well as usage data, to help fix errors, improve the performance of our website and services, and enhance the user experience. We utilize Anthropic's Claude large language model for this purpose, which involves storing your information securely and applying appropriate safeguards.

3. Data Retention and Security:

We are committed to the security and confidentiality of your personal information. We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

3.1. Data Retention:

We retain your submitted data and responses for a period of five days to facilitate the generation of customized documents and to address any technical issues that may arise during this time. After this period, we automatically delete your information from our systems, ensuring that it is no longer accessible.

3.2. Third-Party Sharing:

We will never share your personal information, submitted data, or responses with any third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent. However, please note that in order to provide the service, we utilize Anthropic's Claude large language model, which may temporarily store and process your data. Rest assured, Anthropic's Claude adheres to strict data protection and privacy standards.

4. Your Rights and Choices

4.1. Access and Modification:

You have the right to access, update, correct, or delete the personal information you have provided to us. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 6.

4.2. Consent Withdrawal:

You have the right to withdraw your consent to the collection and processing of your personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for individuals under the age of 14. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we may have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete the information promptly.

6. Feedback

We are committed to continuous improvement. In our upcoming version, we aim to provide industry or job-specific customizations, varied resume templates for download, and faster resume and cover letter generation.

Your feedback is essential in shaping the future of ApplyTo.jobs. If you have any questions regarding the privacy practices of our website and services, or have ideas for future updates, please reach out to our support team at info@applyto.jobs.
